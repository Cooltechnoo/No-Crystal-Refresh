# 💎 No-Crystal-Refresh

A lightweight, high-performance client-side optimization mod that prevents End Crystals from randomly refreshing or resetting their placement animation in your hotbar. Keep your PvP fluid, predictable, and stutter-free.

## ✨ Features

* **Zero Hotbar Stutter:** Stops the frustrating ghost-refresh animation of crystal stacks when spamming or pacing placements.
* **Pure Client-Side Fix:** Completely safe to use on any multiplayer server. It alters only how your client handles the inventory packets visually, giving you a competitive edge without modifying server logic.
* **Performance Focused:** Written with efficiency in mind, using clean injection hooks to ensure absolutely zero impact on your framerate.
* **Plug & Play:** No complicated configuration menus or setups required—just drop it into your mods folder and enjoy a seamless crystal PvP experience.

---

## 🤔 Why use this?

In vanilla Minecraft, rapid actions or slight server latency drops can cause item stacks (especially End Crystals) to "blink" or refresh in your hotbar as if you just selected them. This visual glitch can throw off your timing during intense crystal PvP. 

**No-Crystal-Refresh** intercepts the visual update loop, ensuring your hotbar rendering stays smooth and consistent while keeping your item counts perfectly accurate.
